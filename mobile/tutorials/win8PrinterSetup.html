Windows 8 Front Desk printer setup

  1. At the start screen, begin typing \\resprint.missouristate.edu
  2. A box will appear as soon as you begin typing. Once you're done typing it in, hit enter.
  3. You will be prompted to enter you BearPass Login and password. Be sure to have @SGF after your BearPass Login.
  4. A window will appear with all of the printers listed. Locate the appropriate printer for your building by using the list below. Right-click on the printer and select Connect...

  5. The process of your computer installing the printer may take a few minutes, but it will install the printer and appear in your printer list.
  6. Each time you need to print, you will need to go through the first three steps to ensure you are connected to the server.
