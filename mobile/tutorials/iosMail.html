iOS Mail Setup

  1. On your iOS device, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, and Calendars. Tap Add Account...
  3. Tap Exchange.
  4. Enter the following credentials:
    Email: BearPass Login@live.missouristate.edu
    Server: You can enter outlook.office 365.com, m.outlook.com, or pod51000.outlook.com into this field.
    User Name: BearPass Login@live.missouristate.edu
    Password: Your Office 365 password. If you don't know your Office 365 password, you can change it by going to cams.missouristate.edu
  5. Tap Next
  6. Tap Save to complete the setup.
