Welcome to ResNet

Welcome to the mobile version of the ResNet site. On the mobile version of our site, you will find tutorials for getting hooked up to the Missouri State network and for setting up your computer to print on the residence hall printers.


Should you need assistance with connecting your computer to the network or have problems with your connection throughout the year, we have ResCons (ResNet Consultants) available to assist you in getting the problem resolved.


If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us and we will do our best to ensure you have a positive computing experience here at Missouri State University.
