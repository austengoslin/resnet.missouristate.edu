Windows Phone Wireless Setup

  1. First, unlock your phone
  2. Swipe to the right to and select settings
  3. Tap Wi-Fi on the settings menu
  4. Tap MSU Resnet from the wi-fi menu. If it doesn't appear, ensure you have wireless turned on and are in an area that has wireless coverage.
  5. Type in your BearPass Login and password
  6. Tap done. when it asks you if you want to continue connecting, tap yes
  7. MSU Resnet should show that it has connected
  8. You have successfully connected to wireless!
